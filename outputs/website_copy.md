## Short description
Dojo3 is a Solana-native platform combining a meme token, NFT collection, staking, referrals, and launch services for tokens and Web3 websites.

## Tokenomics
- Total supply: 850,000,000 Dojo3 tokens
- Airdrop: 60% of supply
- Public Sale: 40% of supply
- Token price (public sale): $0.00001

## Airdrop eligibility (summary)
- Eligible if wallet holds > $100 in one of: PumpFun, BONK, Jupiter, WIF, Kamino, Raydium, or a Pudgy Penguins NFT.
- Dojo3 NFT holders are eligible by default.
- Referral reward: 24% (instant to referrer on successful claim)

## Staking & NFT
- Pixel art collection: 999 NFTs
- Stake >= 0.5 SOL to address: HMwy4JHwuLkMMR3q6B3atwZ4oUAGrc3yHtgC7MswWNY1
- Minimum staking period: 3 days
- NFT categories: Warrior, Sniper, Beast

## Fees
- NFT minting: 0.01 SOL (eligible) / 0.2 SOL (non-eligible)
- Any platform transaction: 0.01 SOL
- NFT design & launch: 0.05 SOL
- Token launch service: 0.05 SOL
- Web3 website launch: 0.1 SOL

## How it works (high level)
1. Off-chain eligibility: we scan chains for holders and compute eligibility/weights.
2. Eligible users claim on-chain via an attestation (Merkle proof or signed message).
3. Airdrop distribution occurs from a token treasury under our program rules; referrals are paid instantly.
4. Staking results in NFT mint eligibility after hold period; minting goes through Metaplex.
